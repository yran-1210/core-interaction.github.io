Keshi

Keshi, born Casey Luong, is a Vietnamese-American singer, songwriter, and producer. Known for his lo-fi R&B sound, Keshi rose to fame with a series of self-produced tracks that resonated with a global audience. His music is characterized by intimate lyrics, smooth vocals, and dreamy instrumentals, often blending elements of R&B, indie, and pop. Keshi’s songs reflect themes of love, heartbreak, and self-discovery, creating a deeply personal connection with his fans.

Recommended Songs

  • Drunk: A heartfelt track that explores themes of vulnerability and self-reflection.
  • Blue: A soulful ballad showcasing Keshi's smooth vocals and emotional depth.
  • Right Here: A lo-fi anthem blending melancholic lyrics with a soothing beat.
  • Like I Need U: One of Keshi’s most popular songs, offering a raw portrayal of love and longing.
  • 2 Soon: A reflective track that captures the complexities of moving on after a breakup.
