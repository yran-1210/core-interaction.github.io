Elliot Moss

Elliot Moss is an American singer-songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist known for his hauntingly atmospheric sound that blends electronic, indie, and experimental elements. Gaining recognition with his debut album Highspeeds in 2015, Moss’s music is characterized by introspective lyrics, lush textures, and minimalist production. His soulful voice and evocative storytelling have earned him a dedicated global audience.

Recommended Songs

  • Slip: A breakout track from Highspeeds, this song captivates with its mesmerizing rhythm and emotional depth.
  • Without the Lights: A hauntingly beautiful song that explores themes of loss and self-reflection.
  • Highspeeds: The title track of his debut album, combining intricate beats with Moss’s introspective lyrics.
  • Closedloop: A masterfully produced song that delves into the cyclical nature of relationships and memories.
  • Silver + Gold: A soulful track featuring lush soundscapes and poignant lyrics.
