DPR IAN

DPR IAN, also known as Christian Yu, is a multi-talented artist and producer who blends elements of R&B, alternative pop, and experimental soundscapes. As a founding member of the Dream Perfect Regime (DPR), he has made significant contributions to the world of independent music and visual artistry. His solo work stands out for its introspective lyrics, cinematic production, and unique vocal style. DPR IAN’s songs often explore themes of vulnerability, mental health, and self-discovery, resonating deeply with fans across the globe.

  • Scaredy Cat: A powerful exploration of fear and vulnerability, blending captivating melodies with raw emotion.
